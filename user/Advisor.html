Common Questions
How do I increase my loan limit? +
Maintain consistent EMI payments for 6+ months, improve your CIBIL score, and ensure your income documents are up to date. You can request a limit review from your dashboard.
Can I prepay my loan early? +
Yes! Partial or full prepayment is allowed after 6 months. A minimal prepayment charge of 2% applies. Contact your advisor to initiate the process.
What happens if I miss an EMI? +
A late payment fee applies and it may affect your credit score. Please contact us immediately if you anticipate a payment issue — we can explore restructuring options.
