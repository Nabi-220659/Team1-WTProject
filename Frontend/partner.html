Frequently Asked Questions

Commissions are calculated and credited to your FUNDBEE partner wallet within 48 hours of successful loan disbursal to your referred customer. You can withdraw the balance directly to your linked bank account at any time, with no minimum withdrawal limit.

You'll need a valid PAN card, Aadhaar card for KYC verification, a cancelled cheque or bank statement for payout setup, and a passport-sized photo. The entire process is digital and takes under 24 hours.

No minimum referrals are required to remain an active partner. You earn commission on every successful referral regardless of volume. However, DSA partners who consistently hit monthly targets are eligible for additional performance bonuses.
